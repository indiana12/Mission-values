# Nutrition Overview

[[Life-Systems/01-Fitness/Fitness|← Fitness]]

## Daily Targets (Oct 2025 Metrics)
- Calories: 1,700–1,850 kcal (supports ~0.7 kg/week fat loss).
- Protein: 130–150 g (preserve SMM 48.4 kg).
- Carbs: ~100 g focused around workouts/swim.
- Fats: ~40 g emphasizing omega-3, olive oil, nuts.
- Hydration: 4.5 L water + electrolytes; magnesium + Ashwagandha + Omega-3 at night.

## Meal Framework
| Meal | Components | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Lunch (main) | 200 g lean protein + rice/roti + ½ plate veggies + curd/bone soup | Grilled chicken + rice + sautéed veggies |
| Post-swim snack | Whey + fruit + almonds/peanut butter | Whey shake + kiwi + almonds |
| Dinner (light) | 150 g paneer/eggs + veggies + olive oil | Paneer stir fry + salad |

## Supplement Stack
- Morning: Collagen + lemon water.
- Pre-workout: L-Carnitine 500–1000 mg + coffee (cycle 6–8 weeks on, 2 off).
- Post-workout: Whey + creatine 5 g.
- Night: ZMA + Omega-3 + Ashwagandha.

## Logging Instructions
1. Duplicate the daily template for each day you want to track.
2. Capture meals, macros (estimates), hydration, and notes on cravings or digestion.
3. Tag notable deviations (`#refeed`, `#social-meal`) for later review.
