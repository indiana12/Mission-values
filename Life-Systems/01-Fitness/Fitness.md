## Purpose
Keep movement, nutrition, and recovery aligned with current goals while tracking readiness signals.

## Active Workflows
- **Daily Session Planner** — log intent, completed sets, and RPE; roll unfinished work to tomorrow.
- **Recovery Pulse** — capture sleep, HRV, and mood snapshots to inform training intensity.
- **Fuel Ledger** — note staple meals and supplement timing for quick reference.
